Weather Risk Simulation

VR Urban Planning & Climate Communication

Developed in collaboration with LIRIC and the University of the West of England, this immersive VR experience explored new ways of communicating urban planning and environmental risk to local communities.

Primarily designed for the Igloo immersive projection system, the project simulated weather and climate risk differences under multiple urban planning scenarios across locations in Bristol, Taiwan, and São Paulo.

The experience aimed to prototype more accessible and engaging approaches to public consultation, spatial communication, and environmental awareness.
