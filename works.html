Selected Works

Weather Risk Simulation

VR Urban Planning & Climate Communication

Developed in collaboration with LIRIC and the University of the West of England, this immersive VR experience explored new ways of communicating urban planning and environmental risk to local communities.

Simulated weather and climate risk across multiple urban planning scenarios in Bristol, Taiwan, and São Paulo using immersive Igloo systems.

Solar Panels VR Education Experience

Educational XR Game for Renewable Energy Learning

Created with Hong Kong Baptist University and supported by HK Education Bureau workshops.

Introduced students to solar energy fundamentals, installation logic, and efficiency understanding.

Built primarily for Meta Quest headsets for educational environments.

Solar Panel Data Simulation

Macro-Scale Renewable Energy Visualisation

Simulated large-scale solar energy systems based on coverage density rather than individual placement.

Focused on macro environmental modelling across urban regions such as Easton (Bristol).

Developed for Meta Quest and adapted for Igloo immersive systems.

Eco Community

Interactive Educational Storytelling Experience

Developed with Hong Kong Baptist University for workshops across Korea, Hong Kong, and the UK.

Users experience a fictional eco-community and make decisions affecting sustainability, society, and development outcomes.

Focused on empathy-driven narrative design and participatory learning.
